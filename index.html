Welcome to FillIt - Coloring Book!

This Privacy Policy explains how FillIt (a coloring book game) handles user information. Our goal is to provide a safe and enjoyable experience for all users.

2.1 Personal Information: We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2.2 Non-Personal Information: We may collect limited non-personal information such as device type, operating system, and basic usage data to improve app performance and user experience.

The information we collect is used only to:

FillIt allows users to color images and optionally save them to their device. All coloring data is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to any server.
